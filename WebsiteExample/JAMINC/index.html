OUR PAST

JAM Customs started out as three friends with an expensive taste of cars. With all the trading and movement of many cars between them and others, with the same interest, they decided to start their own company from the ground and make their hobby into a business. After quickly getting success from car deals and trade, the expanding began. The company then decided to relocate in Miami and open their lot to boats. Three years later, with a booming trade and buy of cars and boats, JAM Customs started to customize trucks and get them out to the public.

OUR FUTURE

With plans for the company to keep expanding and eventually get markets across the country and then the globe, we have to further our ties with already booming companys. Therefore, we decided to reach out to Daytona Sports Cars with both parties agreeing to a business deal that will further the success of JAM and attract more business for Daytona Sports. With a collaboration between the next best car dealership in the states and one of the already car dealership leaders, this is expected to be a huge success!
